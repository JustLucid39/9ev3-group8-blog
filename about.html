History

Adamjee Cantonment Public School (ACPS) is a prominent educational institution located in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Established in 1960, the school operates under the jurisdiction of the Bangladesh Army and is situated in the Cantonment area. Over the decades, ACPS has grown from a small institution into a well-respected educational establishment with a large student body and a wide range of programs.

The school's commitment to excellence has remained steadfast, with a focus on developing not only the academic skills of students but also their character and leadership abilities.

Academics

ACPS offers education from the primary level up to higher secondary levels, following the national curriculum of Bangladesh. The school is known for its academic excellence, disciplined environment, and strong emphasis on extracurricular activities.

Our curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills. We offer a variety of subjects including languages, mathematics, sciences, humanities, and arts, ensuring a holistic education for our students.

In addition to regular classes, we offer special programs and workshops to help students excel in competitive exams and pursue higher education opportunities both nationally and internationally.

Facilities

The school provides a well-rounded education with facilities and programs that support both academic and personal development of students. Facilities include:

  • Modern classrooms equipped with the latest teaching aids and technology
  • State-of-the-art science laboratories for practical learning
  • Computer labs with high-speed internet access
  • A well-stocked library with a vast collection of books, novels and poetry
  • Clubs for extracurricular activities including debate, drama, music, and art
  • Health services with a dedicated school paramedic and medical facilities

Goals

Our mission is to provide quality education that nurtures students to become responsible and productive citizens. Our vision is to be a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence and holistic development.

We strive to create a learning environment that is inclusive, supportive, and engaging. Our dedicated faculty and staff work tirelessly to inspire students to achieve their full potential and become lifelong learners.

Achievements

ACPS has a long history of achievements in both academic and extracurricular fields. Our students consistently perform well in national exams and participate in various national and international competitions.

Some of our notable achievements include:

  • Consistently high pass rates in SSC and HSC exams
  • Winning top positions in national science and math Olympiads
  • Successful participation in international debates and model United Nations conferences
  • Award-winning performances in drama and music competitions
  • Numerous sports competition in football, cricket, basketball, and athletics

Our students have gone on to excel in various fields, including medicine, engineering, law, and commerce, making significant contributions to our motherland, Bangladesh.
