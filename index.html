About Adamjee Cantonment Public School

Adamjee Cantonment Public School (ACPS) is a prominent educational institution located in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Established in 1960, the school operates under the jurisdiction of the Bangladesh Army and is situated in the Cantonment area.

ACPS offers education from the primary level up to higher secondary levels, following the national curriculum of Bangladesh. The school is known for its academic excellence, disciplined environment, and strong emphasis on extracurricular activities.

It provides a well-rounded education with facilities and programs that support both academic and personal development of students.
